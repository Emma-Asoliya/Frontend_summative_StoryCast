Our Mission

Archiving the Living Voice

StoryCast was founded in 2023 with a single belief: that the most important cultural knowledge in the world is not held in books or museums rather it lives in people. In the voice of a master weaver. In the hands of a ceramicist. In the songs a grandmother hums while she cooks.

We commission original audio and video stories from artists, craftspeople, musicians, and cultural custodians across the globe, with a particular focus on communities whose traditions have historically been marginalised, overlooked, or actively suppressed.

Every story on StoryCast is free. Every story is permanently archived. And every story is made accessible to the widest possible audience ‚Äî with full transcripts, closed captions, and audio descriptions as standard.

Accessibility

Access for Everyone

StoryCast is built to the WCAG 2.1 Level AA standard. We believe accessibility is not a feature ‚Äî it is the foundation. Below is a full account of how this site meets that commitment.

Media Accessibility

  • Full text transcripts provided for every audio story
  • Closed captions (WebVTT format) on all video content
  • Audio descriptions available for all video stories
  • Fallback text content when browser doesn't support audio/video
  • Downloadable transcripts in .txt and .pdf formats
  • Captions default to ON on all video players

Navigation & Interaction

  • Skip-to-content link at the top of every page
  • Full keyboard navigation ‚Äî no mouse required
  • Visible focus indicators on all interactive elements (3px gold ring)
  • Mobile navigation toggle with proper ARIA expanded state
  • Breadcrumb navigation with aria-current for orientation
  • Escape key closes mobile navigation and returns focus

Semantic Structure

  • Semantic HTML5 throughout: header, main, nav, footer, article, section, aside, figure, figcaption
  • Single, descriptive h1 per page ‚Äî correct heading hierarchy maintained
  • Lists use proper ul/ol/li markup with role="list" where appropriate
  • Definition lists (dl/dt/dd) for structured data
  • ARIA landmark roles on all major regions
  • aria-label and aria-labelledby on all sections and complex widgets

Colour & Typography

  • All text meets WCAG 2.1 AA contrast ratio (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text)
  • Primary text (#3d2b1f on #faf6f0): contrast ratio 12.4:1 ‚Äî exceeds AAA
  • Terracotta accent (#c97d4e on #faf6f0): 3.2:1 ‚Äî used for large text only (pass)
  • No information conveyed by colour alone
  • Focus ring colour (#d4a853 on white): 3.1:1 ‚Äî meets non-text contrast requirement
  • Minimum 16px body text; 14px for supporting labels only
  • Line height 1.65 minimum on body text for readability

Additional Considerations

  • Reduced motion: all animations disabled when prefers-reduced-motion is set
  • Images have appropriate alt text; decorative images use alt="" and aria-hidden
  • Interactive elements have sufficient touch target size (minimum 44√ó44px)
  • Page language declared (lang="en") for accurate screen reader pronunciation
  • No content that flashes more than 3 times per second
  • Tested with NVDA (Windows), VoiceOver (macOS/iOS), and keyboard-only navigation
